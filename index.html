Formula One Racing

Formula One is widely regarded as the most technologically advanced and exciting form of motorsport in the world.

With speeds reaching over 200 mph, F1 cars are the fastest regulated racing vehicles on the planet. The technology that goes into designing and engineering an F1 car is unmatched, with teams constantly innovating to shave off tenths of a second per lap.

F1 also brings together the world’s best drivers who must demonstrate immense skill and endurance to handle these incredible machines at their limits for over 90 minutes per race.

The global reach of F1 and renowned iconic circuits like Monza, Silverstone, and Monaco create an unrivaled atmosphere and prestige.

With billions of fans, intense competition between drivers and constructors, and races at historic tracks in glamorous destinations, Formula One provides an adrenaline-fueled spectacle that makes it the greatest motorsport competition.
